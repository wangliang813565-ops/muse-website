Character Profile

Hala (هلا)

In Arabic, "Hala" means welcome — a greeting of warmth. She is a baby Saker Falcon, the noble raptor of the Arabian Peninsula, reimagined as a plump, gentle character in traditional Emirati dress.

Hala doesn't speak. But her quiet presence radiates home, comfort, and a pride that needs no words. She embodies the spirit of Gulf hospitality — an invitation to slow down and belong.
