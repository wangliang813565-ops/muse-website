## Who We Are

MUSE Information Services L.L.C-FZ is the GCC's first professional designer toy distributor, headquartered in Dubai, UAE. We bridge the gap between global designer toy brands and the Gulf market — offering Dubai ready stock, 2-5 day delivery across all 6 GCC countries, and a curated selection of collectibles tailored to regional demand.

## What We Do

**Distribution** — We source trending designer toys, blind boxes, vinyl figures, and art collectibles from top brands in China and Asia, warehouse them in Dubai, and distribute across the GCC. Retailers get local inventory without the risk and delay of overseas imports.

**Corporate Gifting** — Custom branded merchandise for Ramadan, Eid, National Day, and corporate occasions. From keychains to tote bags to limited-edition figures, we handle concept, production, and delivery.

**IP Development** — Our original character, Hala, is a baby Saker Falcon from Abu Dhabi — the UAE's noble national bird, reimagined as a serene, cozy character in traditional Emirati dress. Hala is available for licensing, brand collaboration, and co-branded products.

## Our Coverage

We serve all 6 GCC countries from our Dubai warehouse:

- **United Arab Emirates** — Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, and all emirates
- **Saudi Arabia** — Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam, and nationwide
- **Qatar** — Doha and nationwide
- **Kuwait** — Kuwait City and nationwide
- **Bahrain** — Manama and nationwide
- **Oman** — Muscat and nationwide

## Why MUSE

**Dubai Ready Stock** — No 25-35 day wait from China. We deliver in 2-5 days from our Dubai warehouse.

**Curated for GCC** — We hand-pick products that match Gulf retail demand, cultural preferences, and gifting occasions.

**Flexible Scale** — Whether you need 10 pieces or 10,000, we handle sourcing, warehousing, and GCC-wide logistics.

**Original IP** — Hala, our homegrown character, brings something unique to the market — a designer toy with genuine Gulf heritage.
